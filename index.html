More about me

I am a French student in computer science at CESI Bordeaux, as well as a web developer with several experiences in this field. I am proficient in HTML/CSS/JavaScript languages, as well as some PHP. I am a fast learner and can quickly adapt to different work environments and frameworks. I am eager to work with you to learn and develop great things!

I have many hobbies in my life. I enjoy doing some sport, like handball, climbing, and going to gym ! I also love playing some videos-games with my friend, drawing, listening to music, and watch anime, movies or series.

I have been doing handball for 4 years, I did 3 years in high school, and I resumed this year 2023. This sport taught me to play as a team, and to take up challenges. Indeed, by resuming handball this year, I was able to play again with my friends from high school, who had also stopped. We had to relearn how to play, while playing in a league far superior to ours. We had a lot of losses, but we still fought all year, until winning, against teams that were twice our level at the start of the year. I play at right-back, which requires high jumps and full-power shots from long range. (In this photo, I am to the right of the person in the red jersey, my goalkeeper).

In April 2023, I took up a challenge with some friends to do 1 month of intense climbing. The challenge was to climb 4 times a week, as well as strength training to improve progression. This challenge taught me to stay consistent, not to lose hope in the face of difficulty and to push my limits. Indeed, the first few weeks my body was exhausted. I could see a clear improvement in only 1 month. At the end of the month, I managed many red runs, and I even managed my first black run!

Projects

During my first internship in 2022-2023 at Brumisphere, I was able to work on different websites, mainly on the front-end. I worked on wordpress, using a simple and manageable template. I made the basic structure in html, then I came to work in css and javascript to get the expected result. This project is a website for Savan, an application that allows you to find service providers to work on vans or motorhomes.

Bordeaux Open Air is a festival that takes place in Bordeaux every summer since 2016. I had the opportunity to work on several different pages of this website, again on wordpress. I was also able to work on the different artists and events pages. For this I was able to work in PHP, in order to create template pages, and to retrieve specific information about the artists/events, and to create all the pages more easily.

Giant Sentinel is a project I worked on during my internship at Programisto in early 2023. I was able to work in html, css and javascript, without a framework. My mission was to create a google chrome extension, which allows to analyse emails, in order to detect if they were phishing attempts or not, and to inform the user. I was able to work on the creation of the extension (login pop-up), as well as on the injection of content in Gmail. This project allowed me to progress a lot in Javascript, in connection portal management, and in API use.
